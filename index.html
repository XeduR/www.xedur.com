Hello!

This entire site is still very much under construction, so don't be surprised if something looks weird or doesn't work perfectly. I should have the time to finally finish everything after this weekend's Ludum Dare 46. Or so I'm hoping.

Most of the projects on this site have been created using the Corona game engine (no relation to Covid-19). The engine is being rebranded to Solar2D in the coming weeks and I'll be changing mentions of Corona to Solar2D after the weekend.

But, now that all that's said:

My name is Eetu Rantanen, but here on the interwebz I often go by XeduR. Welcome to my barely functional site!

I am the founder and lead developer at Spyric, a small mobile game studio in Finland. In addition to game development, I am into graphic design and fiction writing. I also occasionally develop websites or other small web services for my clients.

Here on this site, you'll find HTML5 demos for some of the projects that I've created over the years. Most of these projects have public repositories available on GitHub under the MIT License.

If you want to get in touch with me, you can do so via email or you can find me on the Corona forums.
