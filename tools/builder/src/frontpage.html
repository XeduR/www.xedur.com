Hello!

My name is Eetu Rantanen, but here on the interwebz I often go by XeduR.

I am a jack of all trades. I am a certified data scientist with a couple of years of work experience and I'm a part-time mathematics and statistics lecturer at Estonian Business School in Helsinki. However, since you've ended up on this site, you probably know me better as a game developer and a full stack web developer, who also dabbles in graphic design, creative writing and all sorts of problem solving.

On this site, you'll find interactive online demos for many of the passion projects that I've created over the years. As passion projects, most of them are related to games. The source code for most of these projects is publicly available via my GitHub profile under the MIT License. Similarly, you can find the source code for some of my Solar2D plugins at Spyric's GitHub profile.

While you don't need to credit me in any way for using my projects, I am always happy to hear when my projects are of help to others.
