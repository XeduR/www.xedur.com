Grav-O-Delivery

Grav-O-Delivery is a short and sweet game about delivering interplanetary parcels using the gravitational pull of planets.

How to play:
- Play at your own pace.
- Drag and hold a planet with your mouse to aim.
- Release to fire the parcel.

TIPS:
- The delivery routes are always the same, but their order changes.
- If you want to quickly restart the game, press the reset button in the top right corner.

  • Source available at: https://github.com/XeduR/Solar2D-Projects/tree/master/Games/Grav-O-Delivery
