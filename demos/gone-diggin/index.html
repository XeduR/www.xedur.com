Gone Diggin'

Gone Diggin’ is a simple game about a simple dwarf that simply wants to dig for gold, but to get to that gold, he must go deeper and deeper.

This game was developed for Ludum Dare 48 COMPO, i.e. all code, images, audio, etc., apart from the custom font file, were created by me within 48 hours. The total time spent working on Gone Diggin' was approximately 16-17 hours.

How to play:

- Simply move by using the arrow keys or WASD. Gather as much gold as you can before the time runs out.
- You gain additional time by gathering gold and the timer starts once you’ve collected 3 gold deposits.
- Press Q at any point to restart the game.
