Bomb Tap

Bomb Tap is the 50-line entry in a personal challenge to write complete games in just 50, 100, and 150 lines of code. A bomb bounces around the screen and your goal is to keep it from touching the platforms by tapping to make it jump.

How to play:
- Tap anywhere to make the bomb jump.
- Keep the bomb from touching the platforms for as long as you can.
