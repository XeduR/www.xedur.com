Last Stand

It's time to make your last stand!

The undead are coming at you and you need to delay the inevitable for as long as you can by dodging the enemies and shooting them dead... er. If you run out of bullets, just dash at them, but remember that dashing hurts you too, so stick to shooting!

This game was developed for Ludum Dare 50 JAM, i.e. all code, images, audio, etc., apart from the custom font file and the background audio, were created by me within roughly 13 hours.

Controls:
[MOUSE]: aim & shoot!
[WASD or arrows]: move!
[1,2,3]: select a weapon!
[space]: dash!
